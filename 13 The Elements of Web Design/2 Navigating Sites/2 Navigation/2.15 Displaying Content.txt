2.15 Displaying Content

When dealing with large lists of content, which option is best for websites that contain user-generated content as the focal point, such as Twitter and Facebook?

[Single Answer]

Pagination
Breadcrumbs
Infinite Scrolling


Answer: Infinite Scrolling
Note:
Breadcrumbs are used to help users understand where they are. Think about how Twitter and Facebook deal with their content and how their users view it.
Pagination is best for platforms that intend to satisfy the goal-oriented activities. Think about how Twitter and Facebook deal with their content and how their users view it.
