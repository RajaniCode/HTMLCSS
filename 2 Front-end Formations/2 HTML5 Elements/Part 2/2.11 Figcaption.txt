2.11 Figcaption

We’ve added a caption to the image in our blog post. Mark it up using the appropriate HTML5 element.


index.html

<figure>
  <img src="armando-blontio.jpg" alt="A Famous Armando Blontio Painting" width="640" height="470" />
  <p class="caption">"Crème de la Crème," a famous Armando Blontio painting.</p>
</figure>


Answer:

<figure>
  <img src="armando-blontio.jpg" alt="A Famous Armando Blontio Painting" width="640" height="470" />
  <figcaption>"Crème de la Crème," a famous Armando Blontio painting.</figcaption>
</figure>
