2.10 Figure

We have a self-contained piece of content for a picture in a blog post. Mark it up using the appropriate HTML5 element.

index.html

<div class="image">
  <img src="armando-blontio.jpg" alt="A Famous Armando Blontio Painting" width="640" height="470" />
</div>

Answer:

<figure>
  <img src="armando-blontio.jpg" alt="A Famous Armando Blontio Painting" width="640" height="470" />
</figure>
