2.2 Section

We want to mark up the grouping of related content on our blog with the appropriate HTML5 element. Use an HTML5 element to mark up the content.

index.html

<div class="section">
  <h2>Blog</h2>
  <p>This is the Art Gallery blog.</p>
  <!-- ... -->
</div>

Answer:

<section>
  <h2>Blog</h2>
  <p>This is the Art Gallery blog.</p>
  <!-- ... -->
</section>
