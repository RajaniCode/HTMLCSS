3.4.3 Color Scheme

Bring the design to life with some color. Utilize a complementary color for the highlight and ensure the text is legible on the background.

Hint:

1. Set Main Background color to H: 225, S: 60%, L: 20% and the Main Text Layer to H: 225, S: 50%, L: 90%

2. Set Secondary Background color to H: 225, S: 0%, L: 90% and the Secondary Text layer to H: 225, S: 50%, L: 20%

3. Set Highlight Background color to H: 40, S: 100%, L: 50% and the Highlight Text layer to H: 45, S: 100%, L: 10%
