3.4.5 Font Size

Finally, set proper font sizes throughout the design keeping in mind visual hierarchy.

Hint:

1. The body copy should be set around 16-18px, and the navigation should be the same size.

2. The headline should be 2-3 times larger than the body copy, and the B-head copy should be 1.5 times larger than the body copy.

3. The footer text should be 2-4px smaller than the body copy.
